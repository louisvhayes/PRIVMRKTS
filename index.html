About the platform

A Research Journal Built for Serious Private Markets Practitioners

PRIV•MRKTS is an independent research platform covering the private markets landscape — private equity, hedge funds, venture capital, infrastructure, and macro. Every piece is built as an interactive, data-rich document: not a blog post, not a news feed.

Think of it as a living research library. Company profiles, deal structure explainers, market trend analyses, interactive charts, and deep dives into specific funds, strategies and transactions — all in one place, built to the standard you'd expect from a tier-one research house.

The journal grows with every new entry. The more you explore, the more you find. Each file links to the next. Use the journal as a reference tool, a learning resource, or a research foundation for your own work.

Why PRIV•MRKTS

Research That Respects Your Intelligence

01
No surface-level summaries
Every entry goes deep. Deal-by-deal breakdowns, actual transaction mechanics, verified financial data where it exists, and honest caveats where it doesn't. We don't publish what everyone already knows.
02
Interactive, not static
Charts you can hover, tabs you can toggle, timelines you can scroll. Research built as interactive HTML documents — not PDF slides, not Twitter threads. Designed for deep reading.
03
Structured like a professional database
The journal is organised by sector, deal type, and company — cross-referenced and searchable. Use it as a reference when you need to look something up, or as a curriculum when you want to learn systematically.
04
Built by practitioners, for practitioners
The platform is designed around how finance professionals actually work — dense with data, precise in language, and respectful of the complexity of private markets. No jargon-free dumbing down.
05
Private markets, exclusively
We don't cover public equities, ETFs, or retail investing. PRIV•MRKTS focuses entirely on the private markets ecosystem — the asset classes, mechanics, and players that most platforms ignore or misunderstand.
06
The library grows compound
Each new entry adds to a cross-referenced knowledge base. The more entries that exist, the more valuable each one becomes. A 2024 deal profile connects to a 2022 rates analysis which connects to a 2020 macro entry. Context compounds.
